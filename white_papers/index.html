Managing a Crypto-Currency Portfolio Via Minmax Drawdown Control
with Alice Wang, 2018

abstract An investor with correct beliefs should not experience large drawdowns, but how do we know our beliefs are correct? Minmax drawdown control is an asset allocation framework designed to guarantee low drawdowns against both safe and risky underlying assets. As such it is well suited to deal with novel, or changing assets, such as crypto-currencies.
